Steam Buns

Chinese steamed buns

Ingredients

  • 150 grams Plain flour
  • 11 grams Instant dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 30 grams Fine sugar
  • 320 grams Whole milk
  • 30 grams Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix the ingredients until a unified dough is formed
  3. Shape the dough into a ball
  4. Let the dough proof into a greased bowl until it doubles in size
  5. Divide the dough into equal portions (~50 grams each)
  6. Form balls with the dough and let the balls rest for 30-40 minutes
  7. Steam the dough balls for 8 to 10 minutes
