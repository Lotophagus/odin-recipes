Bread

A crusty loaf of bread

Ingredients

  • 800 grams Bread flour
  • 10 grams Salt
  • 7 grams Dry yeast
  • 540 grams Water

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix the ingredients until a unified dough is formed
  3. Shape the dough into a ball
  4. Let the dough rise into a greased bowl until it doubles in size
  5. Divide the dough in half and fold each piece 5 times into two loaves
  6. Preheat a large pot into the avin to 250C/480F
  7. Put the loaves into the pot, springle them with flour and incise their top with a sharp knife or razor
  8. Bake the bread for 30 minutes at 250C/480F
